Raghuveera Gadyam

A prose composed by Vedanta Desika in the 12th century

Raghuveera Gadyam

Raghuveera Gadyam was composed by a 12th-century poet Sri Vedanta Desika in Thiruvananthapuram, Tamilnadu. He was inspired by the sculptures of Kodanda Rama in the temple (Rama holding his bow) and composed it.

Raghuveera Gadyam describes Lord Rama's qualities of leadership, courage and valour comparing him to a Mahavira --a great warrior. The title Raghuveera Gadyam means "Prose of Rama". If one recites Raghuveera Gadyam, he/she is sure of overcoming all difficulties in life.
